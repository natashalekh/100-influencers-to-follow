Methodology

Source Public data from 5 Apify Instagram scrapers:
1. Instagram Hashtag Analytics Scraper — discover top AI hashtags and trending creators 2. Instagram Hashtag Scraper — extract posts under AI-related hashtags 3. Instagram Profile Scraper — profile data, bios, about pages, related profiles 4. Instagram Profile Scraper — second pass on related profiles for expansion 5. Instagram Post Scraper — posts, carousels, likes, comments 6. Instagram Reel Scraper — reels, views, shares, play counts
Selection Keyword search + related-profile expansion, filtered to verified individual creators posting about AI Excluded News outlets, brands, AI product pages, unverified accounts Metrics Engagement calculated from recent posts & reels. Pinned content excluded from averages. Reel data takes priority over post data where both exist, as it includes share counts. Engagement rate = (likes + comments + shares) / followers Coverage 125 profiles · ~2,500 posts & reels analyzed · ~2,000 unique hashtags extracted Data All data is publicly available on Instagram.
